About Signature Forgery

Signature forgery is a serious crime involving the unauthorized reproduction or imitation of another person's signature.

Types of Forgery

  • Random Forgery: No attempt to replicate the genuine signature
  • Simple Forgery: Basic attempt without practice
  • Skilled Forgery: Practiced imitation after studying the original

Legal Implications

Signature forgery can lead to serious legal consequences including fraud charges, identity theft, and financial crimes. Organizations and individuals must verify signature authenticity to prevent fraudulent transactions.

How We Help
